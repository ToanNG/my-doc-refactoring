Get Started


Extend your practice with technology

Not all encounters need a physical visit. Free up exam rooms and see more patients by shifting appropriate appointments to video.

Increase patient satisfaction and stay current

Research shows that 75% of patients in Singapore want to be provided with digital healthcare services [1] and the evidence shows it works [2].

Improve patient retention

MyDoc provides a manageable way to keep patients engaged with their health and your practice between visits.

Stay connected with your patients outside the clinic

A health diary connects your patients with their care team. This helps you monetize out of hours care and keep an eye on how they are doing between visits.

When you want, make yourself available for video consultations using the online scheduling tool.

HIPAA & PDPA compliant peer collaboration

Communicate with your peers in real time using a service that does not compromise on patient data security.

Send and receive messages, high resolution photos, PDFs and more right from your mobile device.

Seamless patient referrals

Refer patients to your colleagues within MyDoc without filling in any paper forms. Referrals become instant, preventing patients from being lost to follow-up.

MyDoc works with the services that you already use

MyDoc is designed to streamline your existing workflow, integrating automatically integrating lab and radiology results.
